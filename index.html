About



These will be a two-day event targeted to two groups of people. One group will be designers from India who have done a PhD within the past ten years or are about to complete their PhD soon (2013-2023). The second group will be designers across India who might be interested in doing a Design PhD in the future.

The event will invite eminent researchers in the domain, post-doctoral designers from India, and designers interested in doing a design PhD. The contents will include invited talks, experience reports, a showcase of IDC research, including both faculty members and students, and talks and panel discussions by the invitees.

There will be opportunities to explore specific areas of mutual interest and developing a proposal for a PhD. This event will be an opportunity for designers to interact with eminent researchers in the domain.
